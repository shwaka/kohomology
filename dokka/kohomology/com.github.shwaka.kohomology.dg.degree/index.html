Package com.github.shwaka.kohomology.dg.degree

Contains definitions of degrees.

Here we consider degrees in a group. The group is represented by the interface DegreeGroup, and their elements are represented by the interface Degree. For example, IntDegree is the usual Z-grading. We also include definitions of MultiDegree and SuperDegree.
