GMonoid

interface GMonoid<D : Degree, E : GMonoidElement<D>>

A monoid representing basis of com.github.shwaka.kohomology.free.FreeGAlgebra (or something similar to it).

To model the multiplication in com.github.shwaka.kohomology.free.FreeGAlgebra, the multiplication of two elements in GMonoid can be one of

  • an element,

  • the minus of an element or

  • zero.
